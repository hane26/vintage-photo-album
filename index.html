Memories in Time

a chronology of dear moments

— ✦ —
2021

A New Beginning

The year everything quietly started over. Long walks in empty streets, rediscovering what stillness sounds like. Afternoons stretched long and golden, and somehow, that felt like enough.

2022

Open Roads

Road trips with windows down and playlists on shuffle. Roadside diners, misread maps, and laughing until the car drifted into the wrong lane. Every detour turned into the best part.

2023

Gathering Light

The summer of rooftop dinners and late-night conversations. New friendships that felt like they had always existed. Polaroids pinned to walls and pressed flowers between book pages.

2024

Staying Present

A year of small rituals made sacred — morning coffee in quiet kitchens, evening skies that demanded to be watched. Choosing to remember the ordinary because it is, in fact, extraordinary.

A Note

— ✦ —

This album is a small act of remembering — a place where light and time are held still for just a moment. Every photograph is a door. Behind each one lives a particular quality of air, a certain angle of sun, a feeling that was once true and is true again the moment you look at it.
